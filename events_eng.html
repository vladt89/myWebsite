Interesting events


In this section I will not tell you about playing bowling or nice party in the night club. This type of rest is very popular and quite often I participate in it, because in the life should be some variety. This rest is usual for most of the people, but I would like to share my experience from some other events, where sometimes crazy people take part. I advise to visit you at least one of them, because it is really awesome.

  • Tahko MTB 2014 - this year this mountain bike competition held over 2 thousands participants. I improved my time compare to last year and the year before. The route, pictures and results you can find here.

  • Michurinskii marathon 2013 - one of the biggest Russian cross-country mountain bike marathon, where I took part in June 2013.

  • Zelenogorskii triathlon 2012 - the competitions, which include cycling, running and swimming. Report and pictures from triathlon 2012 you can see here.

  • Tahko MTB 2012 - the biggest mountain bike competitions in Finland, which had around 2 thousands of participants in 2012. I was there too! The report and pictures are here.

  • Acoustics - one of the events from the week of the faculty Applied Mathematics and Control Processes, where the students sing and play different musical instruments. Few notes and video for the performance, which was in April 2011, you can see here.

  • State Saint-Petersburg University championship of programming - teams competitions of strongest programmers of the University. October 2010.

  • BikeAdventure 2010 - mountain bike orienteering competitions in Karelia. October 2010. You can read a report from here.

  • Destination - orienteering competitions in the city for the owners of Korean cars. September 2010.

  • Day in the city 2010 - orienteering competitions in the city very similar to Runcity. Exist different categories (foot, bike, rollerblades and so on), bu that time I took rollerblades. May 2010. You can read more about my impressions in the section Hobbies -> Skates and rollerblades.
