TAHKO MTB. 28 of June 2014.


Tänä vuonna minä parannettuin minun aika - nyt minun paras tulos 25 km Tahko matkalle on 1 tunti 37,30 minuttia.

This year I again improved my time - now my best result of 25 km Tahko route is 1 hour 37,30 minutes instead of 2:02 on Tahko MTB 2012 and 1:45 on Tahko MTB 2013. Unfortunately I got only 23 place although in 2013 I was 19. However, this year there were more people in my category - 230 participants. I was very happy about my result, haven't felt down any time and was feeling quite good after the race. My brother got 65 place and made the route in 1:57.
